Add Video to Lens

Add YouTube video transcripts to the Lens library with one click.

Install

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

Add to Lens
If your bookmarks bar is hidden, press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+Shift+B (Mac) to show it.

Usage

1 Go to any YouTube page (a video, your homepage, etc.)
2 Click the "Add to Lens" bookmark in your bookmarks bar
3 A panel opens on the right. Paste one or more YouTube video URLs (one per line), or leave it pre-filled with the current video.
4 Click "Fetch Transcripts" — the bookmarklet extracts transcripts with word-level timestamps directly from YouTube.
5 Review the results, then click "Send to Lens" to queue them for processing.
Transcripts are queued for processing on the server. You can check status at /api/add-video/status.
