Why would automating R&D speed up AI development?

Pros

  • Module structure stays visible while editing a lens.
  • Two docs visible simultaneously (context + detail).
  • Nav is never "wasted space" — you can edit it.

Cons

  • Left pane has two jobs (browse + edit) — more UI density.
  • Modules and LOs are edited in narrow column.
  • Right pane is empty until a lens is picked.
